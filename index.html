Trust Platform

Trust Platform

Droplet offers real use of a cryptocurrency as a payment, it is a decentralized and privacy focused cryptocurrency with simplicity in mind. The "Trust" platform adds real use of a cryptocurrency and will let cryptocurrency holders buy and sell items for Droplet coins.

Easy and Simple to Use

Trust Platform - The Trust Platform enables you to use your cryptocurrency on what you want, localy and internationally. We think of The Trust Platform as Ebay 2.0. Advertise something you want to sell and get payed in Droplet (DPT) To create an account, use your DPT wallet adress and set a password. The wallet will be your ID on the platform.The platform is decentralized so nothing else than a password required.

Reputation Based Platform

TRUST reputation points is a way to promote Loyal and reliable sellers and costumers, and TRUST points is also a way to avoid/warn about fraud and theft. Succesfull Sells or purchaces are rewarded with the reputation points, TRUST The more trust you have, the higher up in the advertisments you get. TRUST is reputation points which are required for you to create or interact with advertisments.

Trust Rewards

Trust Rewards is rewarded when a user on the "Trust" platform has reached 1000 Trust points. 

Trust points is Reputation

Trust points gives you many advantages, more Trust points you have the higher up your listings will be. To reward loyal and hardworking users, Trust points can be exchanged for Droplet coins.

The reward suppy

The Trust rewards supply will decrease over time. When it reaches 0 the only way to fill it up again is to have users buy ads for their listings on the Trust platform. All Droplet coins recieved from ads purchases will refill the Trust rewards supply.

How to earn Droplet

Droplet coins are not mined or staked, they are earned through hardwork, time and effort.

How can I earn coins?

To earn Droplet coins you must first create an account on our "Trust" Platform.
On "Trust" you can create and interact with listings, complete listings or purchases through interactions are rewarded with 1-10 Trust reputation points, depending on the value/risk of the trade. Tasks or quests might also be a way to earn Droplet coins.

How is this safe?

When interacting with a listing, an escrow is opened so comitting fraud is hard. If a trade is disputed, neither of the users can leave with their goods/service until the problem is solved. A user that commits fraud/theft or tries to will recieve a negative amount of Trust points, so that the user can't create or interact with listings ever again. There is a 0 tollerance to thiefs/swindlers on the "Trust" platform. To make it harder for users to create multiple accounts and get away with fraud, new users are limited to interact or create listings with valued under 200 dollars until 10 trades are complete.

DNS - Wallet Username System

DNS - Wallet Username System will make it easier to send and recieve cryptocurrencies. It eliminates the difficulity and risk of typing mistakes. Websites has an ip and a domain, cryptocurrency wallets will have its adress and a domain.

Beginner friendly

DNS - Wallet Username System will enable begginers to send and recieve cryptocurrencies without the need of learning how tx adresses work. Ease of use is what cryptocurrency needs to expand.

Organized & Secure

Think of the DNS - Wallet Username System as when websites started to use domain names insted of ip adresses. Now we are doing it again but with cryptocurrency wallet tx adresses.

P2P transactions

P2P transactions are still intact. The
DNS - Wallet Username System will only enable easier and faster transactions with less risk. We belive this is the future of cryptocurrency. 

DNS - Wallet Username System
In depth explanation

How it will work - A topdomain, like .COIN or .WALLET will be reserved for this infrastructure to be used. You need to buy the domain you want use as a username, for example "John123". all domain names under the topdomain used for this will cost the same amount of money.
Every domain name has a subdomain for every supported cryptocurrency, like bitcoin, ethereum, monero which will look like this: .xmr, .btc and .eth. The subdomains belongs to a specified cryptocurrency.

The domain name you buy, forexample John123, will require you to set a recieving adress for the cryptocurrencies you want to use with the "wallet adress username" service.
So when someone wants to send you a cryptocurrency, lets say Monero(xmr), they type in your username "john123" and chosses the amount and clicks on send. The DNS will then look for the domain "**xmr.John123.coin**". If there is no adress registered for xmr on the domain or none have purchased the domain, then there will be a warning and the funds cant be sent to the username.
When will this be added? - We are reaching out to the world of crypto right now on every social media platform to share the idea and make it a reality.
Currently we could create an exclusive wallet for Droplet which is the only wallet in the world with this feature at the moment, but insted we want to share the idea so that everyone can use the feature and not just Droplet, improving the cryptocurrency network as a whole and increasing the ease and sinking the risk of sending and using cryptocurrencies.


Lets say you want to use bitcoin (btc) and Droplet (dpt)
You only need to:

1. buy the domain you want as a username

2. setup an adress for the subdomains       (btc) and (dpt)

3. let your friends know your username.

Why Droplet?

IN DEPTH EXPLAINATION
  • DECENTRALIZATION - Droplet will have its own decentralized exchange and its own decentralized shopping platform. There is a huge request of decentralized exchanges as the current exchanges are making choices about hardforks of Bitcoin and Ethereum airdrops that many people in the cryptocurrency community doesn't agree upon. The decentralized exchange will be added and open for trading after the end of Droplets public-crowdsale that starts the 1st of January. Decentralization was the idea behind the creation of cryptocurrencies when it was first made, in 2009. We are taking decentralization to the next level with our decentralized platform "Trust" and our decentralized exchange.
  • PRIVACY - Privacy is important in the cryptocurrency space as every transaction is traceable, we will implement Znark technology as soon as we move over to our own blockchain with our own miners and stakers. This will not happen in the near future, but we are already working on a transition from the ERC-20 standard to become an individual Cryptocurrency.
  • TRUST - Droplet will have its own platform called "Trust", on "Trust" you can buy and sell what you want with Droplet. We like to call the "trust" platform "Ebay 2.0". The platform will be decentralized and private, no password or username is required. The platform will supply every new user with a set amount of reputation points, called TRUST points. TRUST points will define your account and other users can see your TRUST balance. The reputation points will make it easier to separate theft and fraud from loyal and trustworthy users, TRUST is also required to create or interact with an advertisement. To gain "TRUST", you have to successfully finish an advertisement, you can gain 1-10 TRUST per advertisement, depending on the price. The more TRUST points you have, the higher up on the list of advertisements you get, its done this way to reward loyal and trustworthy users. When a user has 1000 TRUST or more, the TRUST points can be exchanged for Droplet tokens, with a 1000 for 1 ratio. A user will be punished with a negative TRUST balance if the account is suspected for fraud, this makes the user unable to create or interact with advertisements. 20% of all Droplet tokens will be locked up until we have the platform up and going, these Droplet tokens will then only be used for exchanging TRUST reputation points. The Trust platform is needed as there is thousands of cryptocurrencies, but very few places to use them. The trust platform enables actual shopping with cryptocurrencies. The TRUST points is our way to reward contributors of the cryptocurrency network, and motivate them and newcomers to use cryptocurrencies on a daily basis.
